What's New

New visuals

AoTTG 2 has music with exclusive songs, new animations, SFX and new models for characters and titans.


From UI to Characters: Customization

One of the biggest features of AoTTG 2 is the huge customization that is possible within the game. From how your in-game hud looks like, to the color of the textures from your skin, there are endless options to choose from.

New and remodeled maps

With the addition of many new maps, we also couldn't forget about the old ones, so the classic maps from AoTTG 1 have been remodeled to match the new looks of AoTTG 2!
