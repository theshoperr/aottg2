Features

Marketplace

In-game and in-browser marketplace where you can find all assets related to Aottg 2, from skins, to maps, here is the place to find it all.

Character Customization

Customize your character in-game by accessing the "character customization" menu, where you can distribute your skill points, change hair style, capes and others. Create your custom presets and use them everywhere you want. You are free to create what you want. If you want more customization options, head over to the Marketplace, where you can download skins made by the community.

HUD Editor

On Aottg 2 you will now have the option to customize your in-game hud. You can move, make elements smaller or bigger, hide or show and more! You can now have a better experience while playing by making the game fit your needs. Come check out the feature inside the game!
